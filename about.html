About Paper Quilling

What is it?

The art of paper quilling is a centurys-old craft that involves curling thin strips of paper into coils, which are then pinched into shapes and arranged into patterns.

Materials Needed

Paper quilling is mainly made using thin strips of paper, commonly about three millimeters (or as wide as the border around this.) You also need glue, a corkboard, and pins. You can use paper quilling tools, which are not necessary but make it much easier! A complete set of supplies can be bought at Amazon.

What all can you make?

Literally anything! My favorite things to make are flowers, but you can create anything you can imagine. Most quilled pieces are somewhat flat, but you can evan make 3D pieces. One of the best quilled creations I've ever made is a 3D Christmas elf who stands about four inches tall.
